About Us

Supplify is a disaster relief supply management system designed to ensure the efficient distribution of essential goods to people affected by natural calamities. Our platform prevents the over-supply of unnecessary items and under-supply of critical resources by providing real-time insights into what is needed at each relief camp. With Supplify, suppliers can see the exact quantity of required products in different camps and contribute accordingly. The system streamlines communication between admins, volunteers, and camp heads, ensuring swift action in times of crisis.
